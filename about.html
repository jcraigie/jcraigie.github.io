About me

My name is John Craigie I am a San Diego native who yearns to run away from the constant sunshine of this “wonderfully sunny beach city” and run to somewhere rainy and dreary. I spent two years traveling the west coast and helping communities in Americorps NCCC and fell in love with the Pacific North West. Beautiful Country. I have spent too many years working in corporate security with a brief, deliciously enlightening, foray into culinary school and have now ended up at Dev Bootcamp in their inaugural cohort for San Diego. For fun in my free time I participate in medieval reenactment through the SCA.
